深度学习和集成学习结合的一些最新研究

经过初步的文献查阅，只找到了较少的相关研究，使用的关键词是如下词的组合：

Ensemble, combining, committee, fusion, bagging, boosting, deep learning, deep neural networks

Shalev-Shwartz S.等[1]提出了一种用于DL的Boosting算法：SelfieBoost，这种方法和传统的Adaboost有所不同，SelfieBoost并不构建多个基分类器进行集成，而是在单一的神经网络上使用Boosting系的优化方法达到加速训练的效果。

Ciresan Dan及其研究组等主要针对中文字符识别和路标识别等问题，对多个CNN等深度网络进行集成，集成方法为直接平均[2,3,4,5]或stack层次集成[6]

西工大谢磊等[7]使用online Boosting方法完成目标追踪任务。

Yi Sun等（汤晓鸥组）[8,9]在人脸关键点识别上使用了一个三层层次集成的深度CNN模型。

西交大张春霞等[10]对RBM进行了集成，方法是Bagging的简单集成方法。

从目前的情况看，除了在多分类任务中采用1-vs-all方法集成多个二分类深度模型，其他形式的深度学习模型集成研究似乎非常少。当然，目前的文献查找可能并不完全，后续可能还需要继续查阅。

 

[1] Shalev-Shwartz S. SelfieBoost: A Boosting Algorithm for Deep Learning[J]. arXiv preprint arXiv:1411.3436, 2014.

[2] Cireşan, Dan, et al. "Multi-column deep neural network for traffic sign classification." Neural Networks 32 (2012): 333-338.

[3] Ciresan, Dan, et al. "A committee of neural networks for traffic sign classification." Neural Networks (IJCNN), The 2011 International Joint Conference on. IEEE, 2011.

[4] Meier, Ueli, et al. "Better digit recognition with a committee of simple neural nets." Document Analysis and Recognition (ICDAR), 2011 International Conference on. IEEE, 2011.

[5] Ciresan, Dan Claudiu, et al. "Convolutional neural network committees for handwritten character classification." Document Analysis and Recognition (ICDAR), 2011 International Conference on. IEEE, 2011.

[6] Masci, Jonathan, et al. "Stacked convolutional auto-encoders for hierarchical feature extraction." Artificial Neural Networks and Machine Learning–ICANN 2011. Springer Berlin Heidelberg, 2011. 52-59.

[7] Zhou, Xiangzeng, et al. "An ensemble of deep neural networks for object tracking." ICIP, 2014.

[8] Sun, Yi, Xiaogang Wang, and Xiaoou Tang. "Deep convolutional network cascade for facial point detection." Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2013 IEEE Conference on. IEEE, 2013.

[9] Luo, Ping, Xiaogang Wang, and Xiaoou Tang. "Hierarchical face parsing via deep learning." Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2012 IEEE Conference on. IEEE, 2012.
